Beautiful Poem

The sun sets in the distant sky,
Painting the world in shades of gold.
A gentle breeze whispers by,
As the story of the day is told.

The moon rises, a silver glow,
Casting its light upon the night.
Stars twinkle, as if to show
Their beauty in celestial flight.

Nature's canvas, ever grand,
A masterpiece for eyes to see.
And as I stand on this wondrous land,
I'm filled with awe and harmony.

- John Doe
